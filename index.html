Privacy Policy

AS Weather Application

Last updated: September 2025

Welcome to our Weather Application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our weather application.

Information We Collect

Location Information

Our app collects your location data to provide accurate weather forecasts:

  • Precise Location Data: When you grant permission, we access your device's GPS or network-based location to determine your exact coordinates for weather forecasting.
  • Approximate Location: If you deny precise location access, we may use your IP address to determine your approximate location (city-level accuracy).
  • Saved Locations: We store locations you manually select for quick access to weather information.

Technical Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our app:

  • Device information (type, operating system, browser)
  • App usage statistics and performance data
  • IP address for location approximation (when precise location is not available)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide accurate weather forecasts for your current location or selected locations
  • Improve our app's functionality and user experience
  • Troubleshoot technical issues
  • Understand how users interact with our app to make improvements

Third-Party Services

Our app uses Open-Meteo API to fetch weather data. When you use our app, certain information (including your location coordinates) is sent to Open-Meteo to retrieve weather forecasts. We encourage you to review Open-Meteo's privacy policy to understand how they handle your data.

Data Storage and Retention

We store your preferred locations locally on your device for quick access. We do not store your precise location data on our servers. Location data used for weather requests is transient and not retained after the API call is completed.

Your Choices and Controls

You have control over your information:

  • Location Permissions: You can grant or deny location access through your device settings. Denying location access will limit the app's ability to provide weather information for your current location.
  • Saved Locations: You can add or remove saved locations within the app at any time.
  • App Data: Clearing the app data or uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored information.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
